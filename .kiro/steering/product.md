# Product Overview

Kiro Fitfin AI is a unified health, fitness, finance, and personal growth AI-powered web app with a calm, practical mentor tone. It delivers discipline-focused guidance through clear dashboards, routine nudges, and emergency alerts.

## Core Philosophy

- Discipline, routine, and small improvements over complexity
- Wise senior mentor tone: encouraging but realistic
- Privacy-conscious, AWS-first serverless architecture
- Clear structured data presentation with red emergency alerts only

## Key Features

- **LifeFitFinSync Score**: Unified scoring across health, fitness, finance, and personal growth
- **Health & Diet**: Calorie tracking, macros, hydration, sleep, 4-6 week weight predictions
- **Fitness Planning**: Daily movement targets, weekly schedules, home-based alternatives
- **Study & Skills**: Pomodoro technique, spaced repetition, discipline tracking
- **Finance Sync**: Food waste tracking, expiry alerts, savings optimization
- **Emergency Flow**: Red alerts for threshold breaches with pinned doctor contact
- **Time Planning**: Job-schedule-aware routine planning

## User Experience

- Dark/Night mode with deep gray backgrounds and high-contrast text
- WCAG AA accessible colors with icons + labels (not color-only)
- Dashboard-first output format (always structured)
- Data annotations for input: `{{daily_meals}}`, `{{exercise_minutes}}`, `{{job_schedule}}`, `{{study_goals}}`, `{{weekly_expenses}}`
